Ito Shinsui

He specialized in bijin-ga, depicting stylish and modernized portrayals of women through traditional woodblock printing. His work, such as the "New Forms of Thirty-Six Ghosts" series, reflects changing roles and fashions of Japanese women in the early to mid-20th century. Betsy Westendorp

Betsy Westendorp-Osieck was a Dutch painter, watercolourist, etcher, pastelist and draftsman who was part of the Amsterdamse Joffers painting group.

Victor Nizovtsev

Victor Nizovtsev is a masterful oil painter of theatrical figurative composition, fantasy, landscapes, and still life. While his professional art training occurred in Russia, as an artist Victor is a student of rich and diverse experiences.
